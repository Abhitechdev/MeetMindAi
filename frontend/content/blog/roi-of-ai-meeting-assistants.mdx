## What is an AI Meeting Assistant and How Does It Work?

An AI meeting assistant is a software application that joins your video conferences (like Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams) or records in-person audio, transcribing the conversation in real-time. But transcription is just the baseline. 

The best **AI meeting software** in 2026 uses advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) to understand context. It doesn't just give you a wall of text; it gives you:
1. **Executive Summaries:** A concise, readable overview of the entire meeting.
2. **Action Items:** Automatically extracted tasks assigned to specific individuals.
3. **Key Decisions:** A clear record of what was decided and why.
4. **Sentiment Analysis:** Understanding the tone and engagement levels of the call.

By automating the administrative heavy lifting, AI assistants allow every participant to be 100% present in the conversation.

## How to Choose the Right AI Meeting Software in 2026

With the market flooded with AI tools, how do you choose the right one to maximize your ROI? Here are the key factors to consider:
